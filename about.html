Our Story

How Velocity9 became a leading platform for DevOps and AWS education

From Industry Experience to Education Excellence

Velocity9 was founded in 2022 by Swastik, a DevOps professional with years of industry experience. After witnessing the growing skills gap in cloud computing and DevOps practices, we set out to create comprehensive training programs that bridge the divide between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation.

Our approach combines deep technical expertise with a focus on career outcomes, ensuring that every student not only learns the tools and technologies but also understands how to apply them in real-world scenarios.

Our Mission

To democratize access to high-quality DevOps and cloud computing education, empowering individuals to build rewarding careers in technology regardless of their background or prior experience.

Our Journey

2022 - Foundation

Velocity9 was founded with a vision to provide industry-relevant DevOps and cloud computing training. Our first cohort had 50 students.

Early 2023 - Growth

We expanded our course offerings to include AWS Solutions Architect training and partnered with 50+ companies for placements.

Mid 2023 - Recognition

Reached 1,000+ students milestone and launched our comprehensive study notes platform, Velocity Notes.

Late 2023 - Expansion

Introduced advanced Kubernetes and Docker modules, and expanded our hiring partner network to 200+ companies.

2024 - Innovation

Launched our updated curriculum with 15+ real-world projects and reached 2,500+ students with a 94% placement rate.

Our Values
